Events

  • Cloth Distribution Drive (November 25, 2023):

    Sahyog organized a cloth distribution drive to provide warm clothing to underprivileged communities. The drive aimed to support those in need as winter approached, ensuring they had the necessary clothing to stay warm.

  • Cleanliness Drive at Tapkeshwar Mandir (December 2, 2023):

    Volunteers from Sahyog participated in a cleanliness drive at Tapkeshwar Mandir, Dehradun. The event focused on maintaining the temple's premises and surrounding areas clean and hygienic, promoting environmental awareness among visitors and the local community.

  • Event at Prem Dham (Old Age Home) Dehradun (December 9, 2023):

    Sahyog organized an event at Prem Dham, an old age home in Dehradun. The event included interactive sessions, entertainment activities, and distribution of essential items to bring joy and companionship to the elderly residents.

  • Drive at Maldevta River Side by Sahyog (January 7, 2024):

    Sahyog conducted a cleanliness and awareness drive at Maldevta Riverside. Volunteers engaged in cleaning the riverside and educating visitors about the importance of keeping natural spaces clean and preserving the environment.

  • Blood Donation Drive at IMA Blood Bank, Dehradun (January 20, 2024):

    In collaboration with the IMA Blood Bank, Sahyog organized a blood donation drive. The event aimed to encourage blood donation among the public and raise awareness about the significance of regular blood donations in saving lives.

  • Cloth Distribution Drive (January 27, 2024):

    Another cloth distribution drive was organized by Sahyog to support those in need. Volunteers distributed clothing items to underprivileged individuals and families, ensuring they had sufficient clothing during the colder months.

  • Cloth Distribution Drive (February 18, 2024):

    Sahyog continued its mission to help the needy with another cloth distribution drive. The team distributed clothing items to marginalized communities, focusing on remote and underserved areas.

  • Meetup with SAMADHAN NGO (March 9, 2024):

    Sahyog held a collaborative meeting with SAMADHAN NGO to discuss future partnership opportunities and joint initiatives. The meeting aimed to strengthen bonds between the two organizations and explore ways to work together towards common goals.

  • Workshop Conducted with SAMADHAN by Sahyog (May 20, 2024):

    Following their earlier meetup, Sahyog and SAMADHAN successfully conducted a joint workshop. The workshop focused on skill development and capacity building for community members, fostering knowledge sharing and collaboration between both organizations.

  • Cleanliness Drive with Sahyog Members (May 28, 2024):

    Sahyog organized another cleanliness drive involving its dedicated members. The drive aimed to clean public areas and spread awareness about the importance of maintaining cleanliness for a healthier environment.

  • Plantation Drive (Planting Trees and Plants in Various Places) (June 16, 2024):

    Sahyog volunteers conducted a plantation drive, planting trees and plants at various locations across the city. The initiative was part of Sahyog's commitment to environmental sustainability and promoting green cover in urban areas.

  • Food Distribution Drive by SAHYOG Dehradun's Volunteers (July 11, 2024):

    Sahyog organized a food distribution drive where volunteers provided nutritious meals to the underprivileged. The drive aimed to address hunger and malnutrition, particularly among the homeless and low-income families.

  • Plantation Drive (July 14, 2024):

    Another plantation drive was organized by Sahyog to continue its efforts toward environmental conservation. Volunteers planted saplings and educated the community on the importance of tree plantation for a sustainable future.
